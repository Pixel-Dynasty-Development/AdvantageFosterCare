Building Brighter Futures Since 2010

For over 15 years, we have been dedicated to our mission of building brighter futures for children by providing safe, stable, and nurturing homes.

Our History

Advantage Family Outreach & Foster Care was founded with a simple yet profound goal: to create environments that empower children to grow, heal, and thrive. What started as a small team of passionate social workers has grown into a leading private foster care agency licensed through the Ohio Department of Youth Services. We are proud of our history and the hundreds of families we have supported on their journey.

Our Approach

We believe that every child deserves a chance to succeed. Our approach is built on a foundation of teamwork, compassion, and unwavering support. We work closely with our foster parents, providing them with comprehensive training and 24/7 access to our experienced team. This collaborative model ensures that both children and foster families receive the resources they need to navigate the challenges and joys of foster care.

Our Values

  • Compassion

    Approaching every child and family with empathy and understanding.

  • Integrity

    Operating with transparency, honesty, and accountability in all that we do.

  • Empowerment

    Providing foster parents and children with the tools and support to achieve their full potential.
