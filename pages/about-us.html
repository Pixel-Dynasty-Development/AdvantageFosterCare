About Us

Advantage Family Outreach & Foster Care is a private foster care agency licensed through the Ohio Department of Youth Services. For over 15 years, we have been dedicated to our mission of building brighter futures for children by providing safe, stable, and nurturing homes.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Approaching every child and family with empathy and understanding.
  • Integrity: Operating with transparency, honesty, and accountability.
  • Empowerment: Providing foster parents and children with the tools and support to achieve their full potential.
