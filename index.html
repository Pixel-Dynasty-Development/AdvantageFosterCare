About Us

Advantage Family Outreach & Foster Care is a private foster care agency licensed through the Ohio Department of Youth Services. For over 15 years, we have been dedicated to our mission of building brighter futures for children by providing safe, stable, and nurturing homes. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to grow, heal, and thrive in an environment that empowers them to reach their full potential.

Our organization was founded on the principle that fostering is more than just a service—it's a community. We work tirelessly to support our foster families with comprehensive training, ongoing resources, and 24/7 access to our dedicated team. Our programs are designed to meet a wide range of needs, from traditional foster care to specialized therapeutic and sibling group placements.

At Advantage, we are committed to making a difference, one child and one family at a time. We are proud of the strong relationships we build and the positive impact we have on the lives of children in our community. Join us in our journey to provide hope and a new beginning for a child in need.

Our Vision

To create a world where every child experiences the love, stability, and support of a permanent family, fostering their growth into resilient, well-adjusted adults. We envision a community where foster care is a celebrated pathway to healing and belonging, and where families are equipped with everything they need to make a profound difference.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Approaching every child and family with empathy, understanding, and a deep commitment to their well-being.
  • Integrity: Operating with transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our actions and decisions.
  • Empowerment: Providing foster parents and children with the tools, training, and support to overcome challenges and achieve their full potential.
  • Community: Building strong, supportive networks among foster families, staff, and partners to create a sense of belonging and shared purpose.
  • Advocacy: Championing the rights and needs of children in foster care, ensuring their voices are heard and their futures are protected.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team is comprised of passionate professionals who bring a wealth of experience and a deep commitment to foster care. From our case managers and social workers to our administrative staff and leadership, every member of Advantage Foster Care is united by a common goal: to provide the best possible outcomes for the children and families we serve. We are here to guide, support, and celebrate with you every step of the way.
